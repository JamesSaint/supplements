MCT Oil – Information Summary

Product Overview

  • MCT (Medium Chain Triglycerides): Typically derived from coconut or palm kernel oil
  • Common types: C8 (Caprylic Acid), C10 (Capric Acid), and sometimes C12 (Lauric Acid)
  • Purpose: Quick energy, brain fuel, ketogenic support, metabolism enhancement
  • Form: Liquid oil, odorless and flavor-neutral, used orally or mixed into foods

How to Take

  • Start with 1 teaspoon per day, increase gradually to 1–2 tablespoons
  • Take with or after food to minimize digestive upset
  • Often added to coffee, smoothies, soups, or taken on its own

Why MCTs Work

  • MCTs are rapidly absorbed and transported directly to the liver
  • Converted into ketones, a clean energy source for brain and body
  • Bypass typical fat digestion pathways – no bile or enzymes required

Expected Benefits

  • Increased mental clarity and cognitive energy
  • Improved stamina and endurance
  • Appetite regulation and possible fat burning in keto/low-carb diets
  • Support for gut health (especially C8 variants)

Safety & Dosage Notes

  • Gradually introduce to avoid nausea or loose stools ("disaster pants")
  • Stick to **pure C8** or **C8+C10 blends** for optimal brain and metabolic benefits
  • Not suitable for cooking at high heat – use raw or gently warmed
  • Safe for most people, including ketogenic diets, unless liver disorders are present

Who Might Benefit

  • Those on low-carb, intermittent fasting, or ketogenic protocols
  • People seeking sharper focus, especially in the morning
  • Those with poor fat digestion – MCTs are easily absorbed
  • Anyone using high-intensity training or brain-heavy workdays

Tips for Best Use

Tip Why It Helps
Start slow (1 tsp/day) Reduces risk of loose stools and digestive upset
Add to coffee or smoothies Quick morning brain fuel with minimal prep
Store in a cool, dark place Preserves freshness – no refrigeration needed
Avoid using for frying Low smoke point – breaks down under high heat
While MCT oil is well tolerated, always consult your clinician if you have liver issues or are on medications that affect fat metabolism.
