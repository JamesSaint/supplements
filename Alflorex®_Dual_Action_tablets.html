Alflorex® Dual Action – Information Summary

Product Overview

  • Clinically researched probiotic strains: Bifidobacterium longum 1714® and 35624® (1 billion CFU)
  • Additional ingredients: Calcium carbonate, Pantothenic acid (B5), Vitamin B6
  • Purpose: Support digestion, stress response, and reduce fatigue
  • Form: 30-capsule pack, one-a-day formula

How to Take

  • Take one capsule daily with or without food
  • Can be taken with water, milk, or any non-acidic liquid
  • No refrigeration required – store below 25 °C

When to Expect Results

  • Initial changes (bloating, mild cramping) may occur in the first 2–4 weeks
  • Full effects often develop over 4–8 weeks with consistent use

Safety & Tolerability

  • Generally well tolerated in clinical trials
  • Temporary digestive effects like gas or mild bloating are common
  • No serious adverse events reported

Allergy & Dietary Suitability

  • Free from: Dairy, gluten, lactose, soy
  • Suitable for: Vegetarians, low-FODMAP diets, pregnancy, diabetes

Interactions & Warnings

  • No known interactions with medications or supplements
  • If taking antibiotics, separate doses by several hours
  • Consult a doctor before use if immunocompromised or recently hospitalised

User Feedback Highlights

  • Many users report less bloating and improved energy within 2–4 weeks
  • Some report temporary digestive upset during the first week

Tips Before You Begin

Action Why It Helps
Take daily with or without food Consistency supports gut adaptation
Track symptoms and energy levels Monitor effectiveness and personal response
Separate from antibiotics Preserves probiotic viability
