Supplement Suggestions for James

  • Liposomal Glutathione (250–500 mg daily): Supports detox pathways, brain protection, post-vaccine inflammation regulation.
  • Magnesium Glycinate (200–400 mg): Nervous system calming, muscle relief, improves sleep and bowel regularity.
  • L-Theanine (100–200 mg): Reduces anxiety, enhances focus, synergistic with caffeine or MCT.
  • Omega-3 (EPA/DHA 1000–2000 mg): Anti-inflammatory support for brain, nerves, and joints.
  • N-Acetyl Cysteine (NAC): Lung and liver detox support, glutathione precursor.
  • Alpha-Lipoic Acid (300–600 mg): Neuropathic pain support, antioxidant for nerve tissue and mitochondria.
  • CoQ10 Ubiquinol (100–200 mg): Mitochondrial energy support, especially post-fatigue or injury.
  • Probiotic blend with Saccharomyces boulardii: Gut resilience post-antibiotics or in dysbiosis flares.
Always consult a practitioner before starting new supplements, especially if you’re on medications, managing chronic conditions, or pregnant.
