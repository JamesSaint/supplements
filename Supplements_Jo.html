Supplement Suggestions for Jo

  • Rhodiola Rosea (200–400 mg): Adaptogenic support for high-pressure leadership and stress.
  • Vitamin B-Complex (activated): Mental performance, hormone balance, and stress resilience.
  • Magnesium Threonate (Magtein): Cognitive clarity, deeper sleep, and nervous system health.
  • Astaxanthin (4–8 mg): Skin radiance, antioxidant protection, supports eye and cardiovascular health.
  • Choline (CDP-choline or Alpha-GPC): Mental performance, memory enhancement, neural repair.
  • Lions Mane Mushroom: Nerve growth factor stimulation, mood, and cognitive enhancement.
  • Iron bisglycinate (if needed): Gentle iron replenishment for menstruating women.
  • Evening Primrose Oil (500–1000 mg): Hormone and skin support, especially premenstrual balance.
Always consult a practitioner before starting new supplements, especially if you’re on medications, managing chronic conditions, or pregnant.
